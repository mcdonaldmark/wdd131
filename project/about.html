About Me

My name is Veronica Lara. I have lived in the United States for almost 8 yeas and I love it here. I currently live with my sister in Utah who is helping me fill my dream in creating a more diverse culture through Ecuadorian cuisine.

I grew up with a love and passion for cooking. My mother was a staunt believer in obtaining the richest and highest quality ingredients and turning it into a delicious, homemade meal. That is what I want to share with my Ecuadorian dishes.

I would love for you to come to my restaurant and bask in the delicate aromas and tastes that are native to my country. If you do, I would love to hear and understand your experiences with the foods that I love so much. On my "menu" page you can review the various products I sell. I also have a "contact" page.
